poligloto.es
The project poligloto.es is a child of MAPL (Moscow Academy of Practical Linguistics), the offline language schools’ network
MAPL originated from the TV show "Polyglot," which is a popular language learning television series. The host is the renowned linguist Dmitry Petrov, the author of a unique method for accelerated language learning. After the success of the TV show, the Petrov family created the offline language school network MAPL, now led by
Ilian Petrov.
I entered the project as a co-founder, my duties are business model and strategical marketing.
